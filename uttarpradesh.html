Textile

Banarasi Sarees

Origin: Varanasi (Banaras), Uttar Pradesh

Description: Banarasi sarees are among the finest sarees in India, known for their intricate gold and silver brocade work, fine silk, and opulent embroidery.

Characteristics: Heavy gold work, intricate designs, Mughal-inspired patterns, pure silk fabric.

GI Tag Year: 2009

Textile

Lucknowi Chikan

Origin: Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Description: Lucknowi Chikan is a traditional embroidery art involving white thread work on fine muslin cloth. It's known for its delicate and intricate patterns.

Technique: Hand embroidery with various stitches like tepchi, bakhiya, phanda, murri, and jangira.

GI Tag Year: 2008

Handicraft

Firozabad Glass

Origin: Firozabad, Uttar Pradesh

Description: Firozabad is famous for its glass industry, particularly glass bangles, decorative items, and glassware. The city is known as the "Glass City of India."

Products: Glass bangles, chandeliers, glass beads, decorative items.

GI Tag Year: 2015

Food

Agra Petha

Origin: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Description: Agra Petha is a translucent soft candy made from ash gourd (winter melon). It's a famous sweet from Agra, known for its unique taste and texture.

Varieties: Plain petha, dry petha, chocolate petha, coconut petha.

GI Tag Year: 2017
