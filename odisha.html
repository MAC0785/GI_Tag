Textile

Sambalpuri Saree

Origin: Sambalpur, Odisha

Description: Sambalpuri sarees are handloom sarees known for their traditional tie-dye patterns, especially the 'Bandha' technique. They feature intricate motifs and vibrant colors.

Technique: Ikat weaving technique with traditional motifs like shankha (shell), chakra (wheel), and phula (flower).

GI Tag Year: 2005

Handicraft

Pattachitra

Origin: Puri and Raghurajpur, Odisha

Description: Pattachitra is traditional cloth-based scroll painting from Odisha depicting mythological narratives and folklore. It's characterized by rich colors and bold strokes.

Themes: Stories of Lord Jagannath, Ramayana, Mahabharata, and other Hindu epics.

GI Tag Year: 2008

Handicraft

Konark Stone Carving

Origin: Konark, Odisha

Description: Konark stone carving is a traditional art form known for its intricate sculptures on temples, particularly the famous Sun Temple at Konark.

Features: Detailed stone sculptures, architectural elements, religious and mythological themes.

GI Tag Year: 2014

Food

Odisha Rasgulla

Origin: Odisha

Description: Odisha Rasgulla is a spongy ball-shaped sweet made from chhena (cottage cheese) and semolina dough, cooked in light syrup. Odisha claims to be the birthplace of this sweet.

Characteristics: Soft, spongy texture, mildly sweet, served in sugar syrup.

GI Tag Year: 2018
